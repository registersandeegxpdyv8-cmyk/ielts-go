📌 名词与冠词 a/an/the▾
规则1：a/an 用于泛指，首次提到的单数可数名词
规则2：the 用于特指，双方都知道的事物
规则3：不可数名词和复数泛指前不加冠词
A dog is a loyal animal.（泛指所有狗）
The dog in the park was friendly.（特指那只狗）
Dogs are loyal animals.（复数泛指，无冠词）
❌ I go to the school every day.
✅ I go to school every day.（固定短语，无冠词）
📌 主谓一致▾
单数主语 → 谓语动词加 s/es
复数主语 → 谓语动词不变
特殊：集合名词（team/family）通常视为单数
She works hard every day.
They work hard every day.
The team is ready. / The staff are on strike.
❌ Everyone have a chance.
✅ Everyone has a chance.（everyone = 单数）
📌 形容词 vs 副词▾
形容词：修饰名词，放在名词前或 be 动词后
副词：修饰动词、形容词或其他副词，常以 -ly 结尾
She is a quick learner.（quick 修饰名词 learner）
She learns quickly.（quickly 修饰动词 learns）
He is extremely talented.（extremely 修饰形容词）
📌 比较级与最高级▾
单音节：adj + er / the + adj + est
多音节：more + adj / the most + adj
不规则：good→better→best; bad→worse→worst
Taller than / the tallest
More beautiful than / the most beautiful
This is the best solution available.
❌ more better ✅ better（不可双重比较）
📌 介词使用▾
时间介词：at（时刻）/ on（日期）/ in（月/年/段落时间）
地点介词：at（具体地点）/ on（表面）/ in（内部）
at 8pm / on Monday / in January / in 2024
at school / on the table / in the box
❌ at the morning ✅ in the morning
⏰ 一般现在时 vs 进行时▾
一般现在时：习惯、规律、真理（always/usually/every day）
现在进行时：此刻正在发生（now/right now/at the moment）
I drink coffee every morning.（习惯）
I am drinking coffee right now.（此刻）
Water boils at 100°C.（科学真理）
⏰ 一般过去时 vs 现在完成时▾
一般过去时：过去某个具体时间发生（yesterday/last year/in 2020）
现在完成时：过去动作对现在有影响（ever/never/already/yet/just）
I visited Paris last year.（具体时间）
I have visited Paris three times.（经历，影响现在）
She has just finished her homework.（刚完成）
❌ I have seen him yesterday.
✅ I saw him yesterday.（yesterday 必须用过去时）
⏰ 将来时的多种表达▾
will + V：临时决定、预测
be going to + V：已计划好的意图
现在进行时：确定的安排（有时间地点）
will be + Ving：将来某时正在进行
I'll open the door.（临时决定）
I'm going to study medicine.（已决定的计划）
I'm meeting her at 3pm tomorrow.（已安排）
This time tomorrow, I will be flying to London.
⏰ 过去完成时▾
had + 过去分词：在过去某时间点之前已完成的动作
常与 before/when/after/by the time 连用
When I arrived, she had already left.
He had studied English before he moved to London.
By 2020, scientists had discovered many new treatments.
⏰ 被动语态▾
结构：be + 过去分词（by + 动作执行者）
雅思写作常用：强调结果而非动作者
The report was published last month.
New laws have been introduced to protect workers.
The building is being renovated at the moment.
✨ 让步状语从句▾
Although/Though/Even though + 从句, 主句（从句含动词）
Despite/In spite of + 名词/动名词，主句（后接名词）
Although technology has benefits, it also brings challenges.
Despite the challenges, she succeeded.
In spite of working hard, he failed the exam.
❌ Despite she worked hard, she failed.
✅ Despite working hard, she failed.（despite 后不接从句）
✨ 条件句（真实 vs 虚拟）▾
第一条件句（真实可能）：If + 现在时, will + V
第二条件句（虚拟/不可能）：If + 过去时, would + V
第三条件句（过去虚拟）：If + 过去完成, would have + V
If it rains, I will stay home.（真实条件）
If I were rich, I would travel the world.（虚拟）
If she had studied, she would have passed.（过去虚拟）
✨ 强调句型▾
It is/was + 强调部分 + that/who + 其余部分
Not only...but also...（不仅...而且...）
What + 从句 + is/are + 强调部分
It is education that shapes a child's future.
Not only does exercise improve fitness, but it also boosts mood.
What we need is a comprehensive plan.
✨ 定语从句▾
限制性定语从句：who（人）/ which/that（物）/ whose（所有格）
非限制性定语从句：which（不用that），加逗号，补充信息
The student who works hard will succeed.
The book that/which I read was fascinating.
Climate change, which affects all countries, requires global action.
✨ 倒装句▾
否定副词置首时需倒装：Never/Rarely/Seldom/Not only + 助动词 + 主语
雅思写作高分句型
Never have I seen such dedication.
Rarely does the government admit mistakes.
Not only did he win the prize, but he also broke the record.
🔥 分词短语▾
现在分词短语（-ing）：表主动、同时发生
过去分词短语（-ed）：表被动、已完成
Having studied hard, she passed the exam.
Overwhelmed by the workload, he asked for help.
Looking out of the window, she saw a deer.
🔥 名词性从句▾
that/whether/what/how 引导的从句作主语或宾语
It is + adj + that...（形式主语）
That education is vital is widely accepted.
It is essential that governments take action.
The question is whether this policy is effective.
I believe that technology has more benefits than drawbacks.
🔥 虚拟语气特殊用法▾
suggest/recommend/insist + that + (should) V原形
wish + 过去时（现在虚拟）/ wish + 过去完成（过去虚拟）
as if/as though + 虚拟语气
I suggest that the government (should) invest in education.
I wish I were able to speak five languages.
He talks as if he were an expert.
🔥 情态动词的完成时▾
could have + V-ed：本可以（但没有）
should have + V-ed：本应该（但没有）
must have + V-ed：过去一定发生了
might have + V-ed：过去可能发生了
She could have won if she had tried harder.
You should have told me earlier.
He must have forgotten the meeting.
They might have taken a different route.
🔥 复合句 vs 复杂句▾
并列句（Compound）：两个独立分句用 and/but/so/or 连接
复杂句（Complex）：主句 + 从句（because/although/when/if...）
雅思写作需混合使用两种句型
Compound: The economy grew, and unemployment fell.
Complex: Although the economy grew, inequality persisted.
Complex: Poverty increases when governments cut social spending.
⚠️ 中国学生最常犯的错误▾
以下是雅思考试中扣分最多的错误类型
❌ The informations are useful.
✅ The information is useful.（information 不可数）

❌ Peoples should pay more tax.
✅ People should pay more tax.（people 已是复数）

❌ I am agree with this view.
✅ I agree with this view.（agree 是动词，不用 am）

❌ He suggest that we should...
✅ He suggests that we should...（第三人称单数）
⚠️ 混淆词辨析▾
affect vs effect
affect（v.影响）: Pollution affects health.
effect（n.影响）: Pollution has a negative effect.

rise vs raise
rise（自动词，不接宾语）: Prices rose by 10%.
raise（他动词，接宾语）: The company raised prices.

fewer vs less
fewer（可数名词）: fewer people, fewer cars
less（不可数名词）: less water, less time
⚠️ 雅思写作禁止用语▾
这些词在雅思写作中显得不正式，要替换
❌ a lot of → ✅ a great deal of / numerous / substantial
❌ big → ✅ significant / considerable / substantial
❌ get → ✅ obtain / acquire / receive / become
❌ but → ✅ however / nevertheless / yet / although
❌ so → ✅ therefore / consequently / as a result
❌ also → ✅ furthermore / moreover / in addition
❌ think → ✅ believe / argue / contend / maintain
❌ show → ✅ demonstrate / indicate / suggest / reveal
⚠️ 标点符号使用▾
分号（;）：连接两个相关的完整句子
✅ The economy grew; unemployment fell.

冒号（:）：引出列举或解释
✅ There are three reasons: cost, time, and effort.

逗号（,）：不能单独连接两个完整句子！
❌ It was raining, we stayed home.
✅ It was raining, so we stayed home.
